Tacos

A picture of the tacos you will make

These tacos are delicious at all times of day. They can be eaten by themselves or with some rice and beans.

Ingredients

  • tortilla
  • pork
  • cilantro
  • white onion
  • chilles
  • love

Steps

  1. Prep the pork
  2. chop the onion and cilantro
  3. make chille paste
  4. cook pork in chile paste
  5. use love to put it all together
